About Us

Gaurav G Agrawal & Co. Chartered Accountants was established on 28th December 2007 by our founder member and Managing Partner, CA. Gaurav Agrawal. Over the years, the firm has gained extensive experience in delivering a wide range of professional services, including Taxation, Regulatory advisory, Audit & Assurance, and Management Consultancy.

We are committed to providing reliable, transparent, and value-driven solutions to our clients across various industries. Our team focuses on building long-term relationships, understanding client needs, and offering practical guidance that supports sustainable business growth.

Our Services

Direct Taxes

We have an extensive team of professionals with sound regulatory knowledge, providing complete tax planning, filing, and advisory solutions for individuals and businesses.

Audit & Assurance

Our expert team ensures accurate financial reporting, compliance, and internal control evaluation to help organizations maintain transparency and accountability.

Banking & Finance

We assist clients with financial planning, loan processing, investment structuring, and smooth banking operations to support better financial decisions.

Corporate Law

We provide end-to-end corporate legal services including company formation, compliance, documentation, and regulatory guidance for smooth business operations.

Management Consultancy

Our consultancy team helps businesses improve performance through strategic planning, process optimization, and effective decision-making support.

Indirect Taxes

We offer complete GST compliance services, return filing, audit assistance, and expert advisory to help businesses manage indirect tax responsibilities efficiently.
