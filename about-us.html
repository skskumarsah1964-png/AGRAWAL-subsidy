About Us

Agrawal Subsidy

Agrawal Subsidy — Gaurav G Agrawal & Co. Chartered Accountants was established on 28th December 2007 by our founder member and Managing Partner, CA. Gaurav Agrawal. Over the years the firm has gained immense experience of rendering diverse professional services in the vertical of Taxation, Regulatory, Audit & Assurance and management consultancy.

We have an extensive team of professionals with sound regulatory & professional knowledge and strong business acumen. We aim at applying intelligence and expertise by providing realistic solutions to complicated business scenarios using analytical skills, functional expertise and intensive training.

We recognise that each client is unique with distinct needs, which must be fully comprehended and analysed in all its dimensions before the right service options can be offered. The firm aims to consistently provide customized solutions to each client, drawing on its wide range of practical experience across a multitude of industries.

We believe in developing a special relationship with our clients which forms the basis of our approach and results in consistent relation with many of our clients for decades.

Our Vision

We envision a future where our commitment to serving 10 Lacs business owners globally has fostered widespread financial literacy, enabling them to make informed decisions and thrive in the competitive business landscape. Through our efforts, we aspire to create a ripple effect of economic empowerment, driving prosperity and innovation across diverse industries.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower and support at least 10 Lacs business owners with the knowledge and resources necessary to achieve financial discipline by understanding their own financial statements, and become statutorily compliant. We are dedicated to assisting entrepreneurs in accessing investment incentives to accelerate their journey towards achieving sustainable growth.
